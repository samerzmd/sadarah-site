The core idea

One rating. Every academy. Always climbing.

Most apps lock your progress inside one club. Sadarah gives every player a single skill score — the Sadarah Rating — that travels with them across academies, sports and seasons. It turns every casual match into a meaningful step.

    Portable across academiesJoin a second or third academy and your rating comes with you — no starting over.
  • ⚖️
    Skill-based matchmakingSessions and matches pair players of similar rating, so games stay competitive and fun.
  • 📈
    Every match countsWin or lose, each result moves the number — giving even practice games real stakes.
  • 🌍

Value from every angle

Your rating and tier are a badge of identity. Players check it the way they check a credit score — it’s theirs, and it’s public.

Competition

Live academy and global leaderboards turn every week into a mini-season. There’s always someone just ahead to catch.

📅

Convenience

The academy / club

Full financial visibility — and the retention to back it

For academy owners, Sadarah runs the money side as smoothly as the courts. Subscriptions, wallets and settlements are tracked, reconciled and reported in one place — so you always know exactly what came in, what's owed, and what's renewing.

✓
Financial visibility & reportingEvery payment, wallet top-up and session fee in clear, real-time reports — revenue, outstanding balances and trends at a glance, no spreadsheets to reconcile.

📈

Progress you own

A rising rating and growing match history become a personal investment. Players feel they’re building something — and losing it would hurt.

🧠 Endowment effect: we protect what feels ours.
🏆

Rivals & rank

Seeing someone 30 points ahead is an itch. Leaderboards manufacture friendly rivalry that pulls players back to close the gap.

🧠 Social comparison: rank is a powerful motivator.
🎖️

Milestone badges

First win, win streaks, loyalty, comebacks — each badge is a tiny, collectible celebration that rewards showing up, not just winning.

🧠 Variable rewards: the next badge is always close.
🥇

Tier promotions

Climbing from Bronze to Platinum gives long-term direction. Each tier is a goal big enough to train for over a season.

🧠 Goal gradient: effort rises as the tier nears.
🔔

Timely nudges

Session reminders, “a spot reclaimed,” rating changes and rank shifts bring players back at exactly the right moment.

🧠 Trigger → action: well-timed cues build habits.
🤝

Belonging

Shared sessions, rosters and an academy identity make Sadarah a community — not just a tracker. People stay where they belong.

🧠 Identity & community: the stickiest retention there is.
